# Third-Party Notices

## Hanon-Online Source Material

The local source PDF corpus is mirrored from Hanon-Online:

https://www.hanon-online.com/

The source manifest records per-exercise page URLs and PDF download URLs in
`corpus/source-pdfs-manifest.tsv`.

Hanon-Online describes the downloadable sheets as Hanon educational materials
and states that users may promote Hanon exercises by printing and sharing the
PDFs. The hanon-trainer project uses that material as attributed educational
source material for local score guidance, overlay authoring, and practice
analysis.

The PDF files, score layouts, site images, and site text are not relicensed by
this repository. They are not committed by default. Local mirrored PDFs are
expected under `corpus/source-pdfs/`, and generated score assets are expected
under `corpus/runtime/assets/`.
